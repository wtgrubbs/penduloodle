About Penduloodle

Pendulum painting is a mesmerizing art form where a paint bucket is suspended from a cord and set in swinging motion. As paint streams from a hole in the bottom, the physics of 2D pendulum motion creates intricate, swirling geometric patterns on a canvas below.

Penduloodle simulates this physics-based art, but with a unique twist: the Canvas itself is attached to an independent pendulum! This dual-pendulum setup offers remarkable flexibility and complexity for generating digital art, appealing to those with a joint interest in physics and creative expression.

If you are unfamiliar with this artform, we highly recommend doing an internet search for "Pendulum Painting Videos" to witness real artists practicing it in action!

Controls Guide

Adjust these parameters to experiment with different physical forces and achieve unique patterns.

  • Speed: Adjusts the overall playback speed of the simulation (0.1x to 3.0x).
  • Quality: Increases the mathematical accuracy and pixel resolution of the canvas. Higher quality allows for smoother curves and high-res saved images, but requires more computational power.
  • Length: Sets the physical length of the pendulum cord. Longer cords produce wider, slower swings.
  • Gravity: Controls the gravitational force pulling the pendulum downward. Set to 0 to remove gravity's effect.
  • Damping: Simulates air resistance and friction. Higher values cause the pendulum to lose energy, slowing down and settling toward the center faster.
  • Initial X / Initial Y: Sets the starting spatial coordinates of the pendulum relative to its resting center point.
  • Velocity X / Velocity Y: Applies an initial directional push (velocity) to the pendulum at the exact moment the simulation begins.

Tool Bar Options:

  • Pen Weight: Changes the thickness of the paint trace left on the canvas.
  • Rainbow: Toggles a mode where the pen color smoothly cycles through the entire color spectrum as it swings.
  • Zero All: Instantly resets all initial position and initial velocity sliders to zero for that specific pendulum panel.
